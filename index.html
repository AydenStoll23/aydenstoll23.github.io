About Me

Hello, my name is Ayden, and I’m an entry-level front-end web developer with a strong background in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I love building clean, visually appealing websites.

While my primary goal is front-end development, I also have experience with back-end programming languages like PHP and MySQL, so I can contribute to full-stack projects when needed. I’ve created several projects, such as small Chrome extensions, mods for games, and websites, all of which are available on my GitHub and showcased on this site. I love expanding my skill set, and I am open to learning any programming languages or technologies that are required for a project.

Outside of development, I spend a lot of time gaming, which is something that works well with my passion for web design because of things like user experience, system design, and problem solving.

I recently completed my Associate of Applied Science (AAS) in Software Development at St. Clair County Community College in Port Huron, Michigan. I am eager to begin my career in software development, apply the skills I have learned, and continue growing as a developer. I’m a quick learner and a great problem solver who looks forward to contributing to a development team.
